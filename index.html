Moc Chau Treehouse

One of the best Mai Chau Homestay

Moc Chau Treehouse Homestay is located in Vatt village, Muong Sang district, this place is where the Thai ethnic community live. Coming to Moc Chau Treehouse, you will be immersed in the wild, rustic, poetic space, experience the unique culture and food here. Moc Chau Treehouse is a very quiet place with views of the fields and hills, a great place to relax and enjoy.
